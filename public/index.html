Kodikas means “code” in Greek. An initiative taken by our club to encourage coding culture among students to test and develop their coding skills in a competitive way. This event is organizing by "YASTIKA"-The Tech Club Of MCKVIE and Department Of Computer Science and Engineering, MCKVIE in association with CSI-Student Chapter, MCKVIE.

ROUND DETAILS

ROUND 1

This round will be a Qualifying round.


  • This round will have 30 MCQ questions (1 marks each).
  • 9 questions will have extra weightage as a star question.
  • Time: - 30 minutes.
  • Questions Curriculum:
    • Basic C Questions
    • Debugging
    • Output Printing

Top 25 teams will be qualified to round 2.

ROUND 2

This round will be a Code Marathon round.


  • This round is a marathon round with 7 questions.
  • 1 hr will be provided to each team.
  • Questions Curriculum:
    • C,C++,Python and Java (with some restricted libraries)
    • There will be 3 test case (2 marks) given for each question, one for the output and other two test case to check the code by the invigilator (1 marks each)
    • It will be on the system

Top 10 teams will be qualified to round 3.

ROUND 3

This round will be a Competitive Coding Round.


  • There will be 4 questions in this round
  • Question based on real life
  • No boundage of language
  • 1 hr & 30 minutes will be provided to each team.

Top 3 teams will be the winners.
